Why This Trip

This trip is more than flights and hotels. It’s Ibrahim bringing Haajar home to Beni Khiar — to the town his family comes from, the streets his parents walked, the sea they grew up swimming in. It’s Haajar bringing Ibrahim home to Shahhat — to the Green Mountain, to her family, to the place that shaped her. It’s two days with mama, papa, and Yasmeen in Milan before we go. Two homecomings, two families, one unforgettable summer together.
