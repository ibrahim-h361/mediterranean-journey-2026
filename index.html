Why This Trip

This trip is more than flights and hotels. It’s Ibrahim meeting Haajar’s family in Shahhat for the first time. It’s Haajar discovering Tunisia — a country she’s never visited despite growing up just next door. It’s two days with mama, papa, and Yasmeen exploring Milan together. And it’s the two of us, hand in hand, navigating three countries, two continents, and one unforgettable summer.
